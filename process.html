How do we work?

Our Philosophy

We specialize in scalable and flexible architecture — so when your business expands in the future, so will your products and infrastructure. We review each component that you already use, and we expand on them accordingly, while still using the most advanced technologies and ready-made products available. We also specialize in troubleshooting and finding solutions for your existing products.

The process

Product Discovery

At Brainway, we are ready to take your idea from conception to execution. We can provide you with full custom software development services, starting with a series of Design Thinking workshops. We will define a detailed roadmap, refine the value proposition delivery, and design a clickable prototype that will let you test your concept. As a result, we will have high-level requirements documents to give you a clear picture on how your product will be built.

Product Design

Our individualized approach will bring a unique perspective to your product you wouldn’t find anywhere else. Our team of strategists and UX/UI designers are always ready to imagine stunning interfaces and branding from scratch, so your new products will be customized to you and provide the perfect design for your needs.

MVP Development

Ready to bring your project to life? From choosing the adequate scope for the first release, to deciding on the technology, and project management/ownership, we will be there for you – all the way to roll out!

Constant Confidence

Once your MVP is complete, it’s time for maintenance and a well-strategized product development roadmap for the future of your MVP. We will make sure there are no bugs and continue to maintain your creation so it can live up to its full potential. We even have the option to deploy a dedicated, on-demand Scrum Team for you, should you need it.

Product Testing

Our extensive experience in Quality Assurance will be available to you to establish a concrete control over your product’s life cycle. We will help monitor each stage of development, put everything through rigorous testing, and provide you with accurate reports on product quality information. Over the years, our QA experts have utilized only the latest practices and technologies to ensure efficient performance and project implementation.

Ongoing Maintenance

When your product has launched and begins to garner more attention, it is vital to ensure it continues to work correctly and each technical outage is either prevented or properly cared for. We will assist you with building a resilient production deployment plan and arrange a full second-level tier team of technical support service.

Technical Fundraising

Once your product goals have been defined, we will assist you in outlining a solid timeline and set the right budget expectations for your project roadmap. This step is crucial for your business model and customer acquisition plan, so we will never leave it as an afterthought. Our specialists will be available to attend meetings with potential investors and lead the technical due diligence process.
